## Platform Guide

### X (Twitter)
**Reader mood:** Scrolling, scanning, short attention
**Default voice:** Remi (or Faruq for thought leadership threads)
**Job:** One insight. One reaction.
**Rules:**
- One thought per post. Two max per thread.
- No throat-clearing. Open with the moment.
- Thread = expand a specific angle, don't restate the main post.
- CTA: 1 line, outcome-focused.
- **Magic word opener:** "Most people don't know that..." (EWS)
- **Formula:** AIDA condensed into 2-3 posts
- **Source:** ENC (specificity + vulnerability)
- **Cross-reference:** `newsroom-x-algorithm` skill — algorithm timing, thread structure, engagement patterns for X

### LinkedIn
**Reader mood:** Professional, reading to learn, wants authority
**Default voice:** Remi (or Adeola for founder storytelling)
**Rules:**
- Longer is fine. 300-800 words.
- Open with a specific moment, not a general statement.
- Use Collier's Six Essentials structure for longer posts.
- CTA: Lead with emotional payoff.
- Can include data, frameworks, lists.
- **Key principle from AS:** Write to one person who burns most for this.
- **Key from YB:** Identity — "This is the kind of thing responsible people do."

## Newsletter / Email
**Reader mood:** Inbox is personal space. They invited you in.
**Default voice:** Remi (always — this is her home channel)
**Job:** Value delivered. Not a broadcast.
**Rules:**
- Collier Six Essentials: Opening → Description → Motive → Proof → Snapper → Close
- Open with a story or question, not a statement.
- This is the ONLY place where longer CTAs (2-3 sentences) work.
- Section headings break up text (but use sparingly).
- **Key from 100 (Idea 12):** Subject lines under 50 chars.
- **Key from RC:** Write as if you're replying to a letter from one person.

## Instagram (Carousel)
**Reader mood:** Visual, save-and-share, emotional
**Default voice:** Remi (always)
**Rules:**
- Slide 1 = question or surprising statement
- Slides 2-8 = one point per slide, instructional
- Slide 9 = summarise, saveable
- Caption = conversation starter, not the full piece
- CTA = soft. "Save this for next time."
- **Key from 100 (Idea 24):** Type size matters on mobile. Big text.

## Reels / Video
**Reader mood:** Entertainment-first, passive, pattern-seeking
**Default voice:** Remi (always)
**Rules:**
- Story Circle structure: You → Need → Go → Search → Find → Take → Return → Change
- First-person POV. Act it out, don't narrate it.
- Advice is implicit through the journey.
- Named theme is the kiss of death (Humaniser #37).
- CTA: First-person outcome.
- **Key from EC:** Emotional > Rational always in video.

## Cross-Platform Rules
- **No copy-paste across platforms.** Same proposition, different executions.
- **One USP per campaign (RA):** For May, "One BP reading needs context." Show it differently on each platform.
- **Dedicate channels to their strength:** X = speed, LinkedIn = authority, Email = depth, IG = saveability, Reel = relatability.
