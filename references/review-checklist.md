# Review Checklist — Final Copy Principles Pass

**Run this AFTER the Humaniser pass, before publish.**

## Structural

- [ ] **One USP per campaign.** Does every asset serve the same proposition with different executions? (RA: Reeves)
- [ ] **Different entry point per platform.** No two assets open the same way. (TFPI: Young)
- [ ] **Unique CTA per platform.** No repeated taglines. (SA: Hopkins, CW: Means-End Chain)
- [ ] **No copy-paste across assets.** Check for verbatim sentences between LinkedIn and newsletter. (Humaniser systemic pattern)
- [ ] **Variable arc.** Not every asset follows hook → factors → instructions → warning → CTA. (TFPI: new combinations)

## Voice & Tone

- [ ] **Opens with a moment, not an announcement.** First line is the thing, not the setup. (Humaniser #26, #29)
- [ ] **No named themes.** Let the story demonstrate, not explain, the lesson. (Humaniser #37)
- [ ] **No indirect constructions.** "Not X. Y" → "Y". (Humaniser #28)
- [ ] **No stutter sentences.** Two consecutive sentences saying the same thing. Merge or cut. (Humaniser #27)
- [ ] **No throat-clearers.** Lines that say what's coming instead of starting. (Humaniser #26)
- [ ] **Rule of three used ≤ 2 times per pack.** One well-earned triplet, maybe two. Not six. (Humaniser #10)

## Persuasion

- [ ] **At least one Life-Force 8 desire triggered per asset.** (CW)
- [ ] **Benefit level reached.** Not feature, not advantage. Benefit of the benefit. (CW: Means-End Chain, 100: FAB)
- [ ] **Every claim is specific.** No "many," "some," "various." Replace with numbers. (SA: Hopkins)
- [ ] **Pre-empts the top objection for this reader.** (CW: Inoculation Theory, EWS)
- [ ] **Emotional hook before rational explanation.** Emotion compels, logic justifies. (RC)

## Technical

- [ ] **Asset passes the platform test.** It does what that channel does best (X=snack, LinkedIn=authority, Email=depth, IG=save, Reel=relate).
- [ ] **Subject line under 50 chars** (if applicable).
- [ ] **Visual pairing specified** (if applicable).
- [ ] **CTA is a complete sentence about an outcome,** not a brand slug.
