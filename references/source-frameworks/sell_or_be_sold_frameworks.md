# Sell Or Be Sold — Framework Summary

**Book by Grant Cardone (2010)**

Sales mindset and closing techniques. Applicable for conversion-focused copy.

## Key Frameworks

### 1. The Formula for Hard Sell
- Know your product inside out
- Know your prospect's dominant buying motive
- Present benefits, not features
- Handle objections before they arise
- Ask for the close multiple times
- Create urgency

### 2. 10X Attitude Applied to Copy
- Over-deliver on value in copy
- Make bigger promises (backed by real capability)
- Write with conviction, not hesitation

### 3. Closing Is Not One Event
- Multiple attempts to close
- Each touchpoint moves closer
- Every email, post, and ad is a close attempt

### 4. Time-Based Urgency
- Deadlines work when real
- "This week only" beats "limited time"

### 5. The "Take Away" Close
- Suggest the product may not be for them
- Triggers desire for what might be lost

## General application
- Refill reminder copy should use multiple close attempts
- "This medication may not be right for everyone — talk to your doctor" = reverse close
- Deadline urgency for free delivery offers
