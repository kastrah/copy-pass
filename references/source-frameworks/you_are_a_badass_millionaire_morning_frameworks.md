# You Are a Badass — Framework Summary
# The Millionaire Morning — Framework Summary

## You Are a Badass (Jen Sincero)
Self-help/motivation. Minimal direct copywriting application.
Applicable concepts:
- **Identity shift** — "become the person who [does the thing]"
- **Abundance mindset** — write from a place of "this helps" not "please buy"
- **Action beats theory** — write for the person who's ready to act

## The Millionaire Morning (Lewis Howes)
Morning habits/routines. Minimal copywriting application.
Applicable concept:
- **Consistent massive action** — applies to content cadence. Daily beats weekly.
