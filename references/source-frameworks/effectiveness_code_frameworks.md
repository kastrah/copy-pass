# The Effectiveness Code — Framework Summary

**Book by James Hurman with Peter Field**

About advertising effectiveness — what makes marketing campaigns actually work.

## Key Frameworks

### 1. The Effectiveness Ratio
Short-term sales activation vs long-term brand building. Best ratio = 60% brand / 40% activation.

### 2. Fame > Liking > Relevance
Fame (being talked about) drives more growth than being liked or being relevant.

### 3. Emotional > Rational Creativity
Emotionally driven creative consistently outperforms rational/feature-driven work in long-term business effects.

### 4. Creative Consistency
Changing the ad every month destroys effectiveness. Consistent campaign platform with fresh executions.

### 5. Distinctive Brand Assets
Ownable colors, characters, sounds, or symbols. Consistent use = compound effect.

## General application
- Blend useful education (brand building) with a clear CTA (activation) — 60/40
- Aim for talkability — content people forward, not just read
- Consistent May theme across assets (brand building) with varied executions
