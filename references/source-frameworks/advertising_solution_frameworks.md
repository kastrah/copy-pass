# The Advertising Solution — Framework Summary

**Book by Craig Simpson & Brian Kurtz**

Covers 6 direct response legends and their key principles.

## 1. John Caples: Testing & Headlines
- A/B test everything
- Headlines that work = emotional triggers + specific promise
- Measure by response rate, not creativity

## 2. Claude Hopkins: Specific Claims
- Specific beats general
- Offer service, not just product
- Tell the full story
- Coupons to track everything

## 3. Gary Halbert: The Most Important Technique
- "Find the hottest prospect for your product, then write to them as if they're the only person in the world."
- Know who burns most for what you sell
- Write to ONE person

## 4. Eugene Schwartz: Breakthrough Advertising
- 5 levels of market sophistication
- Match copy intensity to market awareness
- Your copy competes with the reader's existing desires

## 5. Robert Collier: Emotional Persuasion
- Open by fitting the reader's existing train of thought
- Use word pictures
- Appeal to the 6 prime motives
- Get action through emotion, not logic

## 6. David Ogilvy: Brand & Image
- Brand matters even in direct response
- The product is the hero
- Research before writing
- Never write an ad you wouldn't want your family to read
